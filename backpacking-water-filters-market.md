<p><ul><li>Squeeze Filter</li><li>Chemical Purification</li><li>Gravity Filter</li><li>Pump Filter</li><li>UV Purification</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The backpacking water filter market includes several types, each catering to different outdoor needs. Squeeze filters allow users to efficiently filter water by applying pressure. Chemical purification involves treating water with chemicals to kill pathogens. Gravity filters leverage gravity to pass water through a filter, making them hands-free. Pump filters require manual pumping to draw water through the filter, providing a more controlled flow. UV purification uses ultraviolet light to eliminate microorganisms, ensuring clean drinking water in remote areas.</p></p>
