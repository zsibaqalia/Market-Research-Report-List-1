Water treatment focuses on removing contaminants and pollutants from industrial discharges, ensuring compliance with regulatory standards before releasing treated water into the environment. Conversely, water recycling involves reclaiming and reusing treated wastewater within industrial processes, reducing overall water consumption and minimizing discharge. Both approaches are essential for sustainable water management, helping industries lower operational costs and environmental impacts while enhancing resource efficiency.</p></p>
