<p><p>The shrink wrap machine market is primarily divided into two types: semi-automatic and automatic machines. Semi-automatic machines require manual intervention for film feeding and product placement, making them suitable for small to medium-scale operations seeking flexibility and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, automatic machines offer high-speed performance and efficiency, fully automating the wrapping process, making them ideal for large-scale production environments. Both types cater to various industries, ensuring products are securely wrapped and preserved during storage and transportation.</p></p>
