<p><p>The Flat Panel TV (FPTV) market encompasses various technologies, including LCD, Plasma, LED, and OLED TVs. LCD TVs use liquid crystal displays for high-resolution images. Plasma TVs rely on gas and phosphor technology for vibrant colors and deeper blacks. LED TVs, an advanced LCD variant, utilize LED backlighting for improved energy efficiency and contrast. OLED TVs feature organic compounds that emit light, delivering superior color accuracy and flexibility in display design. Each type caters to different consumer preferences regarding performance and pricing.</p></p>
