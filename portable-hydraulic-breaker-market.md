<p><p>The portable hydraulic breaker market features two primary types: triangle type and tower type breakers. Triangle type breakers are compact, lightweight, and ideal for smaller, confined spaces, making them suitable for residential or light construction applications. In contrast, tower type breakers are larger, offering greater power and efficiency for heavy-duty tasks, such as bridge or road construction. Both types are valued for their ability to deliver precise, controlled demolition while being easily transportable and operable in various environments.</p></p>
