<p><ul><li>Direct Current</li><li>Alternating Current</li><li>Pulsed Current</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The welding power supply market is primarily segmented into three types: Direct Current (DC), Alternating Current (AC), and Pulsed Current. DC power provides a steady arc, ideal for various welding applications like MIG and TIG. AC power alternates polarity, making it suitable for aluminum welding and heavy material removal. Pulsed current combines features of both DC and AC, allowing for controlled heat input and reduced distortion in delicate projects. Each type addresses specific welding requirements and enhances efficiency.</p></p>
