<p><p>Material Jetting is an additive manufacturing process that involves the precise deposition of material droplets to create three-dimensional objects. It utilizes a multi-jet modeling technology similar to inkjet printing, where liquid materials are jetted onto a building platform, cured by UV light, and subsequently layered to form intricate geometries. This method allows for high-resolution, complex designs while using a variety of materials, including polymers and metals.</p><p>The Material Jetting Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.4% during the forecast period.
