<p><ul><li>Unscreened Multicore</li><li>Screened Multicore</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Belden Alternative Cables market is divided into two main types: unscreened multicore and screened multicore cables. Unscreened multicore cables consist of multiple conductors without shielding, making them suitable for applications where electromagnetic interference (EMI) is minimal. In contrast, screened multicore cables feature additional shielding to protect against EMI, ensuring signal integrity in environments with higher interference levels. These variations cater to different needs in industries such as telecommunications, broadcasting, and industrial automation, enhancing connectivity and performance.</p></p>
