<p><p>A Gas Insulated Substation (GIS) is a compact and efficient type of electrical substation that utilizes insulating gas, typically sulfur hexafluoride (SF6), to provide electrical insulation and arc extinction. GIS technology offers several advantages over traditional air-insulated substations, including a reduced footprint, enhanced safety, and lower maintenance requirements. These features make GIS particularly suitable for urban areas and locations with space constraints.</p><p>The Gas Insulated Substation Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.5% during the forecast period.
